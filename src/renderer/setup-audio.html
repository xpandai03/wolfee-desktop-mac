Enable System Audio Capture

Required to record both sides of a conversation

1
Install BlackHole 2ch (free, open source)
Click the button below to download it.
2
Create a Multi-Output Device
Open Audio MIDI Setup (search in Spotlight).
Click + at bottom left, choose "Create Multi-Output Device".
Check both "BlackHole 2ch" and your speakers/headphones.
3
Set it as your output
In System Settings > Sound > Output, select the Multi-Output Device.
This routes audio to both your speakers AND BlackHole for recording.
4
Restart Wolfee Desktop
Quit and reopen the app. It will detect BlackHole automatically.

BlackHole is a free, open-source virtual audio driver by Existential Audio.
It creates a virtual audio device that Wolfee uses to capture system audio.
